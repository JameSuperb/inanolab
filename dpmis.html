R&D Proposal Collaboratory
This web application is an unofficial, independent tool developed to help research teams draft proposals following the DPMIS V2 format. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to DOST or any of its agencies, and proposals saved here are not officially submitted. Sign in with your Google account to access and collaborate on shared proposal drafts.
