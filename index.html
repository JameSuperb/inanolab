About Our Laboratory

The i-Nano Research Facility provides cutting-edge analytical services using state-of-the-art scanning electron microscopy (SEM) and energy-dispersive X-ray spectroscopy (EDX) equipment.

With over 15 years of experience in materials characterization, our team of expert analysts delivers precise, reliable results for academic research, industrial R&D, and quality control applications.

We specialize in surface morphology analysis, elemental composition mapping, failure analysis, and contamination identification across a wide range of materials including metals, ceramics, polymers, and composites.

Sample Preparation Guidelines

1

Sample Size

Maximum dimensions: 25mm x 25mm x 10mm. Smaller samples preferred for optimal results.

2

Cleaning

Remove oils, dust, and contaminants. Use appropriate solvents. Avoid touching analysis areas.

3

Documentation

Label samples clearly. Provide detailed description of areas of interest and analysis goals.

⚠️ Important Notes:

  • Avoid samples containing mercury, radioactive materials, or biological hazards
  • Magnetic samples may require special handling
  • Volatile materials should be pre-treated or avoided
  • Contact us for guidance on unusual sample types
