Graphs and Equations

Week Num: 2

Department: Physics

College: Science

School: De La Salle University – Manila

Teacher: Dr. James Salveo L. Olarve

In this activity, you will learn how graphs are used to represent and analyze experimental data. Graphs allow us to visualize the relationship between physical quantities and help us identify patterns that may not be immediately obvious from numerical values alone. You will practice plotting experimental results correctly, interpreting different types of graphs, and transforming non-linear relationships into linear forms. Using these graphs, you will determine the relationship between variables and formulate equations that describe the physical system being studied. This activity emphasizes that graphs are not just illustrations—they are powerful tools for understanding experimental results and verifying physical laws.
